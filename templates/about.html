1. How it started

All of this started in April 2017. I have joined a group studying cs231n course. This is an amazing course about deep learning. I wasn't going to do a final project, but with time I realized that I want to do at least something at the end of it. In several weeks I have incidentally found a site where people could draw a digit and then some model would perform image recognition and immediately show prediction. Thaw was interesting! Building a model and showing prediction accuracy in Jupyter Notebook is okay, but if anyone can draw some digit and receive a prediction is an instant - this is much more impressive.

2. Motivation

So I wanted to do something similar. But doing exactly the same wouldn't be satisfying. Something new was necessary.
